Sumare ERP Software

Sumare ERP Software is a comprehensive business management software, designed to integrate and automate processes such as finance, inventory, sales and human resources. With advanced technology, Sumare ERP helps improve operational efficiency and supports more informed decision-making.

Integrated Financial Management

Automatically manage budgets, expense tracking, and financial reports to ensure data transparency and accuracy.

Efficient Inventory Management

Monitor stock in real-time, optimize procurement, and prevent shortages or excess inventory.

Human Resources and Payroll

Simplify employee data management, work schedules, and payroll processing with an automated and integrated system.

Integrated Financial Management Features

Integrated Financial Management features in Sumare ERP Software usually include:

Record all income and expenses automatically and in an organized manner.

Provides reports such as balance sheet, income statement, and cash flow for accurate financial analysis.

Efficient Inventory Management Features

Efficient Inventory Management features in Sumare ERP Software usually include:

Monitor stock quantities in real time, including storage locations and item availability status.

Manage the receipt of goods from suppliers and the release of goods to customers automatically and accurately.

Notify or trigger automatic orders when stock is approaching the minimum limit.

Organize inventory by category, SKU, or other attributes for easier searching and management.

Enable inventory management across multiple warehouse locations with full visibility of stock at each location.

Human Resources and Payroll

Human Resources and Payroll features in Sumare ERP Software typically include:

Stores complete employee information, including personal data, employment history, and related documentation.

Provides access to employees to view pay slips, apply for leave, or update data independently.

Create and manage work schedules flexibly, including shift and rotation arrangements.

Automatically calculate employee salaries based on working hours, overtime, deductions, and benefits.

Manage various employee benefits, bonuses, and incentives according to company policy.
